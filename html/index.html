Narratives_by_Kabakoo


Crafting new narratives. One story at a time.


When we started Kabakoo, we often faced the problem of finding suitable images for our communication materials in the available image databases. A problem other people in search of Africa-related images surely know. We hence started producing image material ourselves early on. Today, we have more than a thousand images and videos in our archive, and more are added every week. We are sharing some of our pictures and videos with everyone, to use it instead of those safari-style pictures randomly used, for lack of better, on any topic related to Africa. We are sharing real pictures and videos telling real stories. And we are making them freely available to you. Please use them as intensively as you can! So that together we can create and disseminate new narratives.


All the individuals on the pictures are members of the Kabakoo community and as such have accepted that their photos can be used to fulfill our goals which include crafting new and self-owned narratives for and by the African youth.


Don't believe the hype. As most organizations radically working toward epistemic justice, we face a hard time fundraising and are pretty broke. So if our work and mission resonate with you, please support us: www.kabakoo.africa/investing
